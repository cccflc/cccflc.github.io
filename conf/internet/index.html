There are a wide range of manners in which to enhance classroom learning through use of the Internet; so many, in fact, that some may feel overwhelmed by the idea of even beginning to explore this wonderful resource. Today we will demonstrate a variety of ways, from simple to more complex, that Foreign Language instructors can take advantage of the Internet and make it work for them in the classroom.
