We're pleased to announce that the 2019 Annual CCCFLC Conference will be held on SATURDAY, October 19, 2019 at Rio Hondo College in the city of Whittier.
CALL FOR PRESENTATIONS:
What makes CCCFLC conferences so great? Well, apart from our fabulous attendees, we typically have wonderful presenters! If you'd like to present at the 2019 CCCFLC conference, we're always looking for interesting and engaging presentations, particularly in lesser represented languages. We request that all presentations be as interactive as possible.
If you'd like to present, please complete and submit this online presentation application form (which will also help us to describe your presentation adequately in the conference program).

If you have an idea for a presentation, but are not completely sure where you'd like to go with that idea, please still complete an application, and let us know that you'd like some assistance in developing the idea. We're happy to help!
REGISTRATION AND PAYMENT
The conference registration includes breakfast and lunch. On the registration form, you may specify if you prefer a vegan, vegetarian, or non-vegetarian meal. Parking is free.
Payment can be made by credit card or PayPal (online or in person) or by cash (in person only). Please note that we no longer accept checks; we apologize if this causes any inconvenience.
