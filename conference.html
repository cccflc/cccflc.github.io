The 2019 CCCFLC Conference is this SATURDAY, October 19, 2019 at Rio Hondo College!
>>> CLICK HERE TO VIEW THE CONFERENCE PROGRAM
>>> PARKING
You should park in Lot C (free of charge) and walk toward the area of the red triangle on this campus map. You will walk between bldg. SS and BB and when you reach the end of the building (where the Wray Memorial Theater is), turn left and continue walking straight ahead until you see the Campus Inn on your left side. To make it even easier on you, we have taped convenient arrows on the walkway so you really can't get lost. When you enter the Campus Inn, you'll see our check-in table ready to sign you in and give you your name tag. There will be a Mexican-themed breakfast w/chilaquiles, sweet bread, bagels, muffins, etc. and lots of coffee available from around 8:45am, so feel free to come at that time and "break your fast." You will see lots of tables and chairs set up for you to sit down, relax and wait for familiar faces to greet before the announcements and keynote speaker, Jeff Brown, will explain his input-focused language learning method.  
REGISTRATION AND PAYMENT
The conference registration includes breakfast and lunch. Parking is free.
Online pre-registration has closed. Please plan to register the morning of the conference.
