THE 2023 CCCFLC CONFERENCE WILL TAKE PLACE SATURDAY, OCTOBER 21, 2023!

We are pleased to announce that the 2023 CCCFLC Conference will be held in-person at L.A. Valley College on Saturday, October 21st, from 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM.
CALL FOR PRESENTATIONS:
What makes CCCFLC conferences so great? Apart from our fabulous attendees, we typically have wonderful presenters! If you'd like to present at the 2023 CCCFLC conference, we're always looking for interesting and engaging presentations, particularly in lesser represented languages. We request that all presentations be as interactive as possible.
If you're interested in presenting, please complete and submit this online presentation application form. The information that you include will also help us to describe your presentation adequately in the conference program.
If you have an idea for a presentation, but are not completely sure where you'd like to go with that idea, please still complete an application, and let us know that you'd like some assistance in developing the idea. We're happy to help!
PREREGISTRATION:
The conference preregistration form will be available the week of September 4. Registration will include a continental breakfast,  lunch and parking. Preregistration will be $45 for part-time faculty ($55 at the door) and $55 for full-time faculty ($65 at the door). Payment will be available via Zelle, Venmo, PayPal and check.
If you have an idea for a presentation, but are not completely sure where you'd like to go with that idea, please still complete an application, and let us know that you'd like some assistance in developing the idea. We're happy to help!
