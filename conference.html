We're pleased to announce that the 2025 Annual CCCFLC Conference will be held on Satuday, October 18, 2025.

As no campus was able to volunteer to host this year's conference, the 2025 conference will be an online conference similar to 2020 and 2021, with a few small changes:
• There will be no registration fee for attending
• There will be no keynote speaker
• There will be 3 breakout sessions of 1 ½ hours each
• Each session will consist of a 50-minute presentation, followed by 20 minutes of breakout group discussions, and then back together as a group for 20 minutes of shared highlights
• During lunch we will keep the Zoom meeting open so that those wishing to hang out and visit with each other
• The tentative schedule is:
   10:00 - 11:30 a.m. - 1st Presentation(s)
   11:30 a.m. - 12:30 p.m. - lunch
   12:30 p.m. - 2:00 p.m. - 2nd Presentation(s)
   2:00 - 3:30 p.m. - 3rd Presentation(s)
   3:30 - 4:00 p.m. - Round Robin (open conversation)  
CALL FOR PRESENTATIONS:
For anyone interested in presenting, please submit the 2025 Conference Presentation Application at your earliest convenience. You may submit a tentative plan now and edit it later as needed. Thank you.
CALL FOR CONFERENCE HOSTS:

While the 2025 conference will be online, we sincerely hope that in 2026, we'll be able to return to our in-person format, as we truly enjoy spending time with each and every one of you, and know how much you all enjoy seeing each other. Please, speak with your colleagues and administration, and consider hosting in 2026 (or beyond). It’s a wonderful way to spotlight your campus as well as support the CCCFLC.

If you may be interested in hosting future conferences, we welcome you to submit the CCCFLC Conference Host Campus Application at your earliest convenience. – Note, you may submit a tentative application now and edit it later as needed.
