THE 2022 CCCFLC CONFERENCE WILL TAKE PLACE SATURDAY, OCTOBER 22, 2022!

_____________________________________________

  • The CCCFLC was founded in 1992, therefore we plan to have a few surprises to commemorate this special occasion.
  • Presently, the conference will be in-person and held at Mt. SAC on Saturday, October 22nd, from 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM.
  • As per our usual format, there will be a keynote speaker followed by three breakout sessions comprised of 2-3 presentations.
  • Each presentation will be 45 minutes, 30 minutes of presentation + 15 minutes of discussion/Q&A.
  • Interested in presenting? Click here for the 2022 Presentation Application.
