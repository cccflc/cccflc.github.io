CCCFLC CONFERENCE 2020

Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, the 2020 Annual CCCFLC Conference was held virtually on Saturday, October 24, 2020.
Three one-hour presentations, each followed by a discussion, were offered:
Dr. Karima Feldhus, Saddleback College:
"Turning Zoom into your Language Classroom: Everything you Want to Know About Zoom and More!"
A hands-on presentation that shows you all the tricks you need to know to become proficient in the use of Zoom to teach any language class: how to design your own Zoom backgrounds, assign break rooms, create polls, and use the chat and the whiteboard features, and more. Become skilled at adapting any language activity from the brick and mortar classroom to Zoom.
Click here to view Dr. Felhus's presentation
Click here to view the discussion that followed
Click here to access the presentation materials
Dr. Ana Afzali, Citrus College:
"The Black Plague in Spain in the XIV Century"
 How the Black Plague began, how it arrived to Europe through Italy and finally to Spain; its artistic, political and social implications
The presentation was not recorded at the presenter's request
Jeff Brown, Orange Coast College:
"Describe the Situation!  How to get more input and output from your students than ever before!"
A fun way to provide tons of input and get students speaking almost immediately, by telling students mini-stories and then having them "Describe the Situation." Hilarious and super effective!
The presentation was recorded but its location is currently unknown
