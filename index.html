Welcome to the CCCFLC web site!

This site contains information about past, present and future CCCFLC activities. This site is very much a "work in progress", so please feel free to offer suggestions, additions, corrections, etc. We are especially looking for material related to past and/or current conferences: presentations that we can post, photos, etc. Please send any relevant material to: dcabanel@occ.cccd.edu. THANK YOU!

PRE-REGISTRATION IS OPEN FOR OUR FALL 2020 CONFERENCE!
We are very pleased to be able to share with you that pre-registration for our Fall 2020 Conference is now open. The conference will take place virtually on Saturday, October 24, 2020, between 9:00 A.M. and 4:00 P.M. Registration is FREE and includes access to all three sessions. See the Conference page and/or the latest newsletter (sent out October 9, 2020 and also available here).
